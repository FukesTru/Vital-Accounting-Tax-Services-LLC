# Vital Accounting & Tax Services LLC — Design Brief

## Three Approaches Considered

### 1. Quiet Meridian
Deep navy and warm charcoal with muted gold accents. Serif display headings, generous whitespace, and asymmetric column layouts. Probability: 0.07

### 2. Slate & Ivory
Slate-gray primary with ivory backgrounds, fine rule lines, and a monospaced accent typeface for numbers. Probability: 0.04

### 3. Obsidian Counsel (CHOSEN)
Near-black backgrounds with crisp off-white text, a single warm-gold accent, and editorial typography. Feels like a private wealth management firm — not a storefront tax preparer. Probability: 0.09

---

## Chosen Approach: Obsidian Counsel

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Private Finance / Editorial Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
1. Near-black canvas with deliberate light — every element earns its place.
2. Gold accent used sparingly as a signal of precision and value, never decoratively.
3. Generous vertical rhythm; sections breathe and never compete for attention.
4. Typography does the heavy lifting — no illustrative clutter.

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: deep obsidian `#0D0F14` — authority, seriousness, premium
- Surface: slightly lighter `#141720` — card and panel backgrounds
- Off-white: `#F2EFE9` — warm, not sterile; primary text
- Muted text: `#8A8A96` — secondary copy, labels
- Gold accent: `#B8965A` — CTAs, dividers, highlights; conveys precision and value
- Border: `#252833` — subtle structural lines

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric editorial grid. Hero uses a left-weighted text column with a right-side visual panel. Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and slightly lighter surface cards. No centered hero blobs.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin gold horizontal rule preceding section headings
2. Large, light-weight numeral or letter used as a background watermark in select sections
3. Serif display font (Playfair Display) for headlines; clean sans (DM Sans) for body

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Restrained and confident. Hover states use subtle gold underlines or border reveals. No bouncing, no pop-ups. Scroll-triggered fade-ins at 20px offset.

**Animation:**
- Entrance: `opacity 0→1, translateY 20px→0`, 500ms ease-out, staggered 80ms per item
- Button hover: border color shifts to gold, text brightens, 180ms ease-out
- Nav: background transitions to `#0D0F14/95` with backdrop-blur on scroll, 200ms

**Typography System:**
- Display / H1–H2: Playfair Display, 700, tracked at -0.02em
- H3–H4: DM Sans, 600
- Body: DM Sans, 400, line-height 1.7
- Labels / Caps: DM Sans, 500, letter-spacing 0.12em, uppercase

**Brand Essence:**
Structured tax strategy for high-income New Jersey taxpayers — for those who need clarity, not just compliance.
Personality: Authoritative · Precise · Discreet

**Brand Voice:**
Headlines are declarative and direct. CTAs are invitations, not commands.
Example headlines:
- "Your tax situation deserves more than a filing."
- "Clarity begins with a conversation."
Ban: "Welcome to our website", "Get started today", "Best in class", guaranteed savings language.

**Wordmark & Logo:**
A geometric mark — a bold "V" formed by two angled lines with a thin gold horizontal bar bisecting the apex. Clean, architectural, no serifs in the mark itself. Used at 40px height in nav.

**Signature Brand Color:** Gold `#B8965A`

## Style Decisions
- No emojis anywhere on the site.
- No fake testimonials, reviews, ratings, or credentials.
- No email address displayed publicly.
- All CTAs push toward consultation request or phone call.
- "Serving clients in…" language only for location references.
- Placeholder sections marked "TBD / pending confirmation" where info is missing.
